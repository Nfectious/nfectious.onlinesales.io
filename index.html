Alibaba Trade Assurance Automating the Process Making Money on TikTok

1. What is Trade Assurance?

Trade Assurance is a service offered by Alibaba that protects both buyers and sellers. It ensures that if the supplier fails to meet the terms of the order, such as delivery times or product quality, you can get your money back. This makes it a great choice when you're working without upfront capital, as it reduces the risk of purchasing products.

2. Search for Products

Start by browsing Alibaba’s search bar. Type in keywords related to products you’re interested in selling. For example, if you want to sell accessories, type "fashion accessories." Then, refine your search by using filters:

  • Trade Assurance: On the left side of the search results, you’ll see a checkbox for "Trade Assurance." Select this to only show products that come with Trade Assurance.
  • Minimum Order: Filter for products with a lower minimum order quantity (MOQ) so you don't commit to large orders. This lets you start small.
  • Supplier Location: If you want faster shipping or lower customs fees, filter by suppliers in the U.S. or Europe.

  • Long-Term: As you grow your social media following and product lineup, you could potentially make thousands a month—especially as you build customer loyalty, tap into trends, and add new products to your offerings.
